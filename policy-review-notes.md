# Policy Documents Review - December 6, 2025

## Overall Assessment
The policy documents are well-structured and protect the company's interests appropriately. They cover essential legal requirements for an e-commerce food business in India.

## Terms & Conditions Review
**Strengths:**
- Clear acceptance of terms clause
- Self-pickup responsibility clearly stated
- Prepayment requirement protects against order abandonment
- GST breakdown (2.5% CGST + 2.5% SGST) is accurate
- Cancellation policy is reasonable (before preparation = full refund)

**Recommendations:**
- Consider adding a "Governing Law" clause (Tamil Nadu/India jurisdiction)
- Add "Dispute Resolution" section (arbitration or court jurisdiction)
- Add "Limitation of Liability" clause to cap damages
- Add "Force Majeure" clause for unforeseen circumstances

## Privacy Policy Review
**Strengths:**
- Clearly identifies Thamarai Foods as the data controller
- Lists all data collected (personal + automatic)
- Mentions Razorpay for payment processing
- Good structure following standard privacy policy format

**Recommendations:**
- Add data retention period (how long data is kept)
- Add user rights section (access, correction, deletion)
- Add cookie policy details
- Add third-party service providers list (Razorpay, analytics, etc.)
- Add contact details for privacy-related queries

## Refund Policy Review
**Strengths:**
- Clear eligibility criteria for refunds
- Reasonable non-refundable situations
- Protects company from frivolous refund requests

**Recommendations:**
- Add specific refund processing timeframe (e.g., "within 7-10 business days")
- Add refund method (original payment method)
- Consider adding photo evidence requirement for quality issues

## Shipping & Delivery Policy Review
**Strengths:**
- Clear delivery area specification
- Distinguishes between outlets (T Nagar for delivery, Velachery for pickup only)
- Extended delivery zone with additional charges mentioned
- Contact number provided for delivery queries

**Recommendations:**
- Add estimated delivery times
- Add order tracking information
- Add what happens if customer is unavailable at delivery

## Summary
The policies are generally well-written and protect the company. The main additions recommended are:
1. Governing Law & Jurisdiction clause
2. Limitation of Liability clause
3. Data retention & user rights in Privacy Policy
4. Specific refund processing timeframes

These are optional enhancements - the current policies are legally sound for operations.
